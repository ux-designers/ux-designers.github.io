How to document SAP Solution Portfolio

Solaris is a browser-based application with which SAP employees to document the SAP Solution Portfolio. The Solutions are structured by the Business Capability Model, a model which represent a high-level enterprise architecture (HR,Finance,Commerce). Later in the consumption, it helps SAP customer to easily find a fitting SAP Solution for their specific Industry/Line of Business problem.

Simply give tobi a command and he will do his best to help you.
