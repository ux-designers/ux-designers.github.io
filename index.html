We are The Tools Team at SAP Walldorf.

Our identity

Who we are

We are a team with about 70 people working within SAP, designing and building a variety of products and services; some for internal use, some for external use. We focus on many topics and we love to solve design challenges.

It’s really important to us that our process emphasizes solving real problems for people, rather than building features.
